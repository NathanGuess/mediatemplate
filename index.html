About Superman

Born as Kal-El on the planet Krypton, Superman was sent to Earth as an infant moments before his homeworld’s destruction. Raised as Clark Kent in Smallville, Kansas, he grew up to stand as a beacon of hope, truth, and justice.

Powers & Abilities

  • Super Strength — Lifts immense weights and defeats cosmic threats.
  • Flight — Flies faster than a speeding bullet.
  • Heat Vision — Emits focused beams of solar energy.
  • X-Ray Vision — Sees through objects (except lead).
  • Invulnerability — Nearly indestructible and immune to most harm.
  • Super Speed — Can move faster than sound.

Legacy

Since his debut in 1938, Superman has become a global icon; representing courage, morality, and the belief that anyone can be a hero. His “S” stands for hope, inspiring generations across comics, film, and culture.
